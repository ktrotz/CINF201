Albany, NY Facts
  • Albany is the oldest continuously settled American city.
  • Washington Park was originally a cemetery. About 40,000 bodies had to be reinterred at the Albany Rural Cemetery.
  • Aaron Burr, who duelled Alexander Hamilton to the death, lived in Albany.
  • Alexander Hamilton was married in Albany at First Chruch.
  • The country's oldest pulpit and weathervane resides at First Church.
  • The SUNY Administration Building houses the country's largest operational weathervane.
  • Author Herman Melville lived downtown near First Church and the Palace.
  • There used to be tobaggan races down Madison Avenue.
  • There was going to be a highway behind Empire State Plaza that connected I-90 to the South Mall Arterial to the Thruway at Exit 23. It was going to run partly beneath Washington Park.
